Welcome to our house in France! It's an old Breton farmhouse dating from the 18th Century, with thick granite walls, oak beams, and many other original features.

Set in the small, rural hamlet of Bourgeal, the house is only 3 miles from the nearest market town and 45 minutes from the coast. It's well placed for anyone seeking peace and quiet, or a more active, outdoors-type holiday.

The house faces south and looks out over our small front garden and the hamlet. At the rear, there's a lovely set of old, stone steps leading up to the first floor from our larger back garden.

Indoors, we've retained as many of the original features as possible. These, combined with vintage and handmade furniture and accessories, lend a homely, simple feel to the place. We've also introduced some modern home comforts to make your stay a pleasant one.
