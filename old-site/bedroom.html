Bedroom

The main bedroom looks out over the front garden and hamlet. It has a simple, farmhouse feel, with its exposed stone windowsill and white painted matchboarding.

It's a lovely room, furnished with vintage accessories, a comfy double bed and an old, reclaimed, cast iron fireplace.

In the summer, you can lie in bed and watch the housemartins darting about outside.
