Outside

There are two gardens at the house. The smaller one, in front of the kitchen, is equipped with a cafe-style table and chairs set. The larger garden at the back also contains a barn where you'll find wood for the stoves, a stone barbeque and an old, rustic oak picnic table and benches - perfect for outdoor living.

Surrounded by fields and woodlands, you can take a walk down quiet country lanes straight from the back door, with barely a vehicle to disturb you.

Depending on the season, why not forage for blackberries, hazelnuts or sweet chestnuts along the way?
