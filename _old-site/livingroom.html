Living Room

The living room, just off the first floor entrance hall, has sturdy oak beams, chestnut flooring and whitewashed walls. The main feature has to be the floor-to-ceiling granite fireplace with its super efficient wood burning stove. There's an ample supply of logs and kindling in the barn for you to use.

Equipped with two comfy settees, an Ercol armchair and a table and chairs for four, this is a welcoming room to relax in. We've also provided a selection of board games, books, maps and sketch pads/paints for your enjoyment.

Vintage mirrors and a cheerful mix of cushions and throws all add to the cosy farmhouse feel.
